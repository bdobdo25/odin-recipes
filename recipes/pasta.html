Pasta

We also used this recipe religiously throughout the pandemic, dropping off batches of pasta at our neighbors.It may seem a little daunting at first but practice makes perfect. After your first run of making homemade pasta with these helpful tips, you will become an absolute pro in no time. Promise.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups semolina or 00 flour* (all-purpose flour can be substituted)
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with dough hook attachment, beat the flours, eggs, olive oil, salt and water on medium-low speed until a soft, smooth ball of dough is formed, about 5-10 minutes. If the dough is too dry, add more water as needed. The dough should feel elastic and slightly tacky to the touch.
  2. Working on a lightly floured surface, knead the dough 3-4 times until it comes together.
  3. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and let stand at room temperature for at least one hour or in the refrigerator overnight, letting it come to room temperature prior to using.
  4. Working on a lightly floured surface, divide dough into 4 equal pieces.
